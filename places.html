  • Bratislava castle - One of the most majestic in Slovakia. Overlooking the Danube River, this castle is over 1000 yearls old and has been rebuilt many times.
  • Old Town - Oldest part of the city. It includes many churches, embassies, the riverfront, restaurants and shops.
  • St. Martin's Cathedral - A 13th century gothic structure built in the Romanesque style.
  • Primatial Palace - One of the most beautiful buildings in the city and has been the site of many important events, including royal coronations and the signing of treaties.

  • Grassalkovich Palace - Slovakia's White House.
  • Devin Castle - A 5th century castle destroyed by Napolean's troops in 1809, and now serves as a museum.
  • The Slovak National Theatre - Opera & Ballet House
  • Novy Most (New Bridge) and Observation Deck - A bridge over the Danube River, boasts a spectacular view of the city from the restaurant at the top.
